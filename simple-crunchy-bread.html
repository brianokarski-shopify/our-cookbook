Simple Crunchy Bread

A recipe by Brian

Prep Time 10 min
Rise Time 12-18 hrs
Bake Time 45 min

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1Â¾ teaspoons salt
  • Â½ teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1Â½ cups warm water

Instructions

  1. Mix the dough: In a large bowl, whisk together flour, salt, and yeast. Add warm water and stir until a shaggy dough forms. No kneading needed!
  2. Let it rise: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it sit at room temperature for 12-18 hours. The dough will double in size and have bubbles on the surface.
  3. Shape the loaf: Flour your hands and a work surface. Gently shape the dough into a ball by folding the edges underneath. Don't deflate it too much!
  4. Second rise: Place dough on parchment paper, cover with a towel, and let rise for 1-2 hours until puffy.
  5. Preheat with the pot: Put a Dutch oven (with lid) in the oven and preheat to 450Â°F (230Â°C) for 30 minutes.
  6. Bake covered: Carefully lower the dough (on parchment) into the hot pot. Cover and bake for 30 minutes.
  7. Bake uncovered: Remove the lid and bake another 15 minutes until deep golden brown and crusty.
  8. Cool and enjoy: Let the bread cool on a wire rack for at least 20 minutes before slicing. Listen for the crackle!
