Simple Crunchy Bread

A recipe by Brian

Prep 15 min
Rise 1.5 hrs
Bake 40 min

Ingredients

  • 2Â¼ tsp (9g) active yeast
  • 1 tsp (4g) honey
  • 1Â¼ cup (300g) warm water
  • 1Â½ tsp (10g) salt
  • 3 cups (360g) 00 flour

Instructions

  1. Activate the yeast: Combine yeast, honey, and warm water in a large bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Mix the dough: In a separate bowl, mix together the salt and 00 flour. Dump into the yeast mixture and stir until combined.
  3. Flour the dough: Lightly flour all sides of the dough.
  4. First rise: Cover and let sit for 1 hour.
  5. Shape the dough: Pull and fold each corner of the dough, going around twice (8 folds total).
  6. Preheat: Place your Dutch oven in the oven and preheat to 460Â°F.
  7. Second rise: While the oven preheats, let the dough sit for 30 minutes.
  8. Bake covered: Carefully place dough in the hot Dutch oven. Bake with the lid on for 30 minutes.
  9. Bake uncovered: Remove the lid and bake for another 10 minutes until golden brown and crusty.

Notes

00 flour: This Italian flour gives you that perfect chewy texture and crispy crust. You can find it at most grocery stores or online.

No Dutch oven? Any oven-safe pot with a lid works. The trapped steam is what creates that bakery-quality crust.
