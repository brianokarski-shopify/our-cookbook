Simple Crunchy Bread

A recipe by Brian

Prep 15 min
Rise 1.5 hrs
Bake 40 min

Ingredients

  • 2Â¼ tsp (9g) active yeast
  • 1 tsp (4g) honey
  • 1Â¼ cup (300g) warm water
  • 1Â½ tsp (10g) salt
  • 3 cups (360g) 00 flour

Simple Crunchy Bread

Instructions

  1. Activate the yeast: Combine yeast, honey, and warm water. Let sit 5 min until frothy.
  2. Mix the dough: Mix salt and 00 flour, then dump into yeast mixture. Stir until combined.
  3. Flour the dough: Lightly flour all sides.
  4. First rise: Cover and let sit for 1 hour.
  5. Shape: Pull and fold each corner, going around twice (8 folds total).
  6. Preheat: Put Dutch oven in oven and preheat to 460Â°F.
  7. Second rise: Let dough sit 30 min while oven preheats.
  8. Bake covered: Place dough in hot Dutch oven. Lid on, 30 min.
  9. Bake uncovered: Remove lid, bake 10 more min until golden.

Notes

00 flour gives you that perfect chewy texture. Find it at any grocery store.

No Dutch oven? Any pot with a lid works. The steam creates the crust.
