Green Herby Salad

A recipe by Alex

I've become known as the ‚Äúsalad lady‚Äù among my friends, who often request that I bring a salad to their dinner parties. The magic is really in the herbs, salt, and lemon. Often I'll add a little bit of shallot to the dressing, and let it sit in the lemon to mellow it out. The best part of salad is that there are a ton of options and you can make it to your taste.

Here's one of my favorite combinations:

Ingredients

  • 4 cups greens (little gems, arugula, butter lettuce, or baby spinach)
  • Leaves and tender stems of your favorite herbs, like cilantro, basil, parsley, chives. My go tos are mint and dill, or even just dill.
  • Calevestrano olives, chopped
  • Really good feta
  • Persian cucumbers, sliced
  • Pistachios (if you like crunch)
  • 1 Avocado, diced

Dressing

  • 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon of diced shallot
  • Flaky salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Light olive oil

Instructions

  1. Add everything to a bowl
  2. Add dressing and combine right before serving
